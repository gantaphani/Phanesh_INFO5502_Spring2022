at issue in this appeal is whether an individual bringing an action on behalf of himself and others under the unfair competition law ucl bus,et and the labor code private attorneys general act paga,et must bring his representative claims as a class action.
we shall conclude that the ucl requires that a representative claim be brought as a class action because the ucl requires compliance with the class action provisions of code of civil procedure section bus.we shall further conclude that the paga expressly allows a person to prosecute a representative claim without requiring that it be brought as a class action 
factual and procedural background 
petitioner jose arias brought this action against angelo dairy,and luis,maria,and joe angelo hereafter referred to collectively as angelo dairy.arias was a former angelo dairy employee,who alleged he was not compensated for overtime wages,and that he received no meal periods or rest breaks during his shifts.he alleged numerous other labor code and regulatory violations,and alleged the housing angelo dairy provided its employees was not habitable.in addition to seeking damages from angelo dairy for the harm he alleged he suffered,arias complaint sought damages and injunctive relief in his representative capacity for the interest of other current and former employees of angelo dairy.
arias brought his representative claims pursuant to two statutes the ucl and the paga.angelo dairy moved to strike the causes of action purporting to state claims in arias representative capacity on the ground arias did not comply with the requirements for pleading a class action.respondent granted the motion to strike,and arias filed this petition for writ of mandate.we issued an alternative writ and stayed the proceedings pending resolution of this petition.
discussion 
iarias ucl claims 
the ucl prohibits business practices that are unlawful,unfair,or fraudulent.hudgins neiman marcus group,th,d.as enacted and until the passage of proposition in the ucl authorized a person to bring an action for relief on his or her own behalf or for the benefit of the general public to restore to any person in interest any money or property,real or personal,which may have been acquired by means of unfair competition.bus,stats,ch.
as pertinent,former business and professions code section provided actions for any relief pursuant to this chapter shall be prosecuted exclusively in a court of competent jurisdiction by the attorney general or upon the complaint of any board,officer,person,corporation or association or by any person acting for the interests of itself,its members or the general public.stats,ch.
thus,under the prior law a private plaintiff had standing to sue to obtain relief under the ucl in the interests of itself even in the absence of an injury to the plaintiff.stop youth addiction,lucky stores,cal th,d.further,a private plaintiff was also authorized to act for the interests of the general public,to bring a representative action not certified as a class action in which a private person was the plaintiff and sought disgorgement restitution on behalf of persons other than or in addition to the plaintiff.kraus trinity management services,cal th,fn,d.
in,the voters passed proposition.section one of the initiative measure set forth findings that the unfair competition laws were being misused by private attorneys who filed frivolous lawsuits where no client had been injured and who filed on behalf of the general public without accountability to the public or adequate court supervision.see historical and statutory notes,west ann.bus.supp.foll.to remedy that circumstance the proposition altered the standing and procedural requirements for bringing a ucl action.as relevant here,business and professions code section now requires that a plaintiff have suffered damages,and business and professions code section requires compliance with the provisions of section 
section deals with two topics joining a party as a defendant if his consent to be a plaintiff can not be obtained,and representative or class actions.it provides if the consent of any one who should have been joined as plaintiff can not be obtained,he may be made a defendant,the reason thereof being stated in the complaint and when the question is one of a common or general interest,of many persons,or when the parties are numerous,and it is impracticable to bring them all before the court,one or more may sue or defend for the benefit of all.
the supreme court has stated that section is based on the equitable doctrine of virtual representation daar yellow cab cal d.it is the primary statutory authority for class actions in california.cohelan on california class actions ed,city of san jose superior court cal d.
arias argues that the plain language of proposition is clear and unambiguous,and that it contains no requirement that a representative suit be brought as a class action.we shall conclude that although proposition does not on its face require a representative claim to be pled as a class action,it requires that the claim comply with section,which is commonly understood to authorize class actions.the requirement that a representative claim comply with section makes plain that a representative ucl claim must be pursued as a class action.to the extent that proposition presents any ambiguity,we resolve it by the indicia of the voters intent.that intent,as set forth in the official ballot pamphlet,was that representative claims under the ucl be brought as class actions.
the objective of statutory construction is to determine the intent of the lawmaker,giving the words of the statute their usual and ordinary meaning.moran murtaugh miller meyer nelson,llp cal th,d.when the statutory language is clear,we need go no further.if,however,the language supports more than one reasonable interpretation,we look to a variety of extrinsic aids,including the objects to be achieved,the evils to be remedied,legislative history,the statutory scheme of which the statute is a part,contemporaneous administrative construction,and questions of public policy.ibid.the rules of statutory construction for initiative measures are the same as those for enacted statutes.people bustamante th,fn,d.
in this case,arias is correct that the language of proposition as set forth in business and professions code section does not expressly require that a person who has suffered injury as a result of unfair competition bring representative claims as a class action.instead,the statute requires such claims comply with section.likewise,section does not contain the term class action.however,the second half of section,allowing one person to sue or defend for the benefit of all where the question is one of common interest,of many persons,or where the parties are so numerous as to make it impracticable to bring them all before the court,has historically been interpreted to authorize one or more individuals to bring a class action.
the supreme court has interpreted the second half of section as permitting two types of representative suits class actions and actions by an association on behalf of its members.californians for disability rights mervyn,llc cal th,fn,d.an early case construing section stated that the subject of the last half of the section is class suits,and that the section provides for class suits barber california employment stabilization commission d,barber.in a landmark class action case,daar yellow cab cal d,the supreme court accepted without discussion that section authorizes class actions,and explained that in construing the second half of the statute,it uniformly has been held that two requirements must be met in order to sustain any class action there must be an ascertainable class citations and there must be a well defined community of interest in the questions of law and fact involved affecting the parties to be represented citations.id.at.judicial opinions in this state have repeatedly held that section is the statutory authorization for class actions in california.richmond dart industries,cal d,code of civil procedure section authorizes class action suits in california bell farmers ins.exchange th,d
