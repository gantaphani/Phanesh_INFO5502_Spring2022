prima facie case 
the people exercised three peremptory challenges of the potential jurors.all three of the challenges were to jurors with hispanic surnames jurors,and.defendant contends the trial court erred in failing to find a prima facie case of group bias on the part of the prosecutor.
test for a prima facie case 
the california constitution and the united states constitution prohibit the exercise of peremptory challenges solely because of group bias.batson kentucky,d people wheeler cal d.when a defendant believes the prosecution is exercising peremptory challenges in violation of the constitution,the trial court must follow this procedure first,the defendant must make out a prima facie case by showing that the totality of the relevant facts gives rise to an inference of discriminatory purpose.citations.second,once the defendant has made out a prima facie case,the burden shifts to the state to explain adequately the racial exclusion by offering permissible justifications for the strikes.citations.third,i f a explanation is tendered,the trial court must then decide whether the opponent of the strike has proved purposeful racial discrimination.citation.johnson california,supra,at.at,fn.omitted.
in people johnson cal th,d,the california supreme court held that the test for establishing a prima facie case of group bias is that the objector must show that it is more likely than not the other party peremptory challenges,if unexplained,were based on impermissible group bias.id.at,d.
limited to the question regarding the applicable test to establish a prima facie case,the united states supreme court granted certiorari and in johnson california,supra,d johnson,held that california more likely than not standard is an inappropriate yardstick by which to measure the sufficiency of a prima facie case.id.at.at.the court found the appropriate standard to be that a defendant satisfies the requirements of batson first step by producing evidence sufficient to permit the trial judge to draw an inference that discrimination has occurred.id.at.at.
